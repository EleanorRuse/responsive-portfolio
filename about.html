Hi, I am
Eleanor Ruse

Frontend Developer, based in London, UK .

Originally from Dartmoor, Devon. At the age of 18, I moved to London with just three months rent in my pocket and went out to try and find a job.

Five years later, I am a Campaign Planner with 4 years of experience working in Media. I specialise in digital campaign management and being part of the API/ACLT Programmatic testing team, where I first found an interest in Tech.

In December 2020 during lockdown, I decided to broaden my horizons and try something new. I started a 'basic introduction to coding' course with Shecodes. From there my interest in the world of coding snowballed into months of courses and individual creations that lead me to a keen interest into the world of Front-end development.

I am always ready for a challenge and my love for coding was originally defined when I spent hours trying to fix a small piece of code. I then came to the realisation that I would never feel the relief I did in that exact moment again if I stopped coding! I am lucky to have now discovered what I love to do.
